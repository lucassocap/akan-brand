| Product Red | ~#C83232 | Packaging Chew+ (rojo saturado) |
| Product Brown | ~#5C3A2E | Packaging Drop bottles |

### Tipografia
| Font | Familia | Uso | Peso | Size |
|------|---------|-----|------|------|
| TiemposHeadline | Serif | Headlines, display | Bold 700 | 36-60px |
| Apercu | Sans-serif | Body, UI, nav | 300/400/700 | 14-20px |
| ApercuMono | Monospace | Labels, precios | 400 | 12-14px |
| Knockout | Display | Numeros oversized | Bold | 48px+ |

### Botones
| Tipo | BG | Text | Height | Radius | Font |
|------|-----|------|--------|--------|------|
| Primary | #E22631 | White | 60px | Pill | Bold, uppercase, 0.75rem, tracking 0.05rem |
| Secondary | #231E20 | White | 44-60px | Pill | Bold, uppercase |
| Ghost | Transparent | Dark | Auto | — | Regular + arrow icon |

### Grid & Breakpoints
- **Container:** max-width 1440px
- **Columnas:** 12-col grid
- **Gap:** 1.5-2.25rem
- **< 669px:** 1 columna (mobile)
- **669-947px:** 2 columnas (tablet)
- **948-1219px:** 3 columnas (desktop small)
- **≥ 1220px:** 4 columnas (desktop full)

### Border Radius
- Cards: 8-12px
- Botones: pill (50px+)
- Imagenes testimoniales: circulo completo
- Product images: 0 (cutout sobre fondo)

### Animaciones
- Fade in: 0.2-0.3s ease
- Loading spinner: rotation 750ms
- Hover: transform suave
- Hero: parallax background
- Testimonial overlay: semi-transparent dark
